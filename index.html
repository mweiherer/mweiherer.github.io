Maximilian Weiherer
maximilian dot weiherer at fau dot de

I am a first year PhD student at FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg, advised by Prof. Bernhard Egger and Prof. Marc Stamminger. I am part of the Chair of Visual Computing.

My research interests are broadly centered around computer vision and image processing, both with applications in medicine. I am particularly interested in statistical shape modeling, non-rigid surface registration, and Gaussian processes.

I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science from OTH Regensburg in 2019 and 2021, respectively. Before joining FAU, I worked several years at the Regensburg Medical Image Computing (ReMIC) Lab as a Student Researcher, where I was advised by Prof. Christoph Palm. I also spent some time at Fraunhofer IIS as part of the Image Processing and Medical Engineering Department.
